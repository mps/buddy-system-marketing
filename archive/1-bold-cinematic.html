Platform Features

Everything you need to pair with precision.

One tool to post availability, discover partners, manage requests, and track your sessions.

📅
Post Availability
Share your open time windows and let teammates come to you. Set your skills, preferred session length, and topics.
🔍
Browse & Discover
Filter open slots by team, skill, or time. Find the exact engineer you need, exactly when they're available.
🤝
One-Click Requests
Send a pairing request in seconds. No DMs, no calendar links, no thread archaeology.
✅
Instant Confirmations
When your request is accepted, both engineers get a confirmed session with all the details they need.
📊
Sessions Dashboard
Upcoming sessions, history, and stats — all in one place. Know exactly what's on your pairing calendar.
🏷️
Skill & Interest Tags
Tag your profile with the tech you know and the topics you love. Get discovered by engineers who need your expertise.
